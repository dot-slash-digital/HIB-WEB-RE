Our Products

Hibiscus Extract Powder

Certified Organic

We specialize in supplying certified organic Hibiscus extract powder to the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries.

Our latest version, PhytoHib™ 100, is 100% pure concentrated Hibiscus and is instantly soluble in ice cold water. The resulting crystal clear beverage possesses the robust flavor and dark burgundy-red color of real Hibiscus and will closely match brewed Hibiscus tea made from boiled dried flowers.

Organic Hibiscus Extract Powder has a 1-year shelf life and is packaged in 25 kg poly-lined small fiber drums for easy storage.

Hibiscus Concentrated Liquid Extract

Certified Organic

PhytoHib™ 40 is our 40 Brix concentrated liquid organic Hibiscus extract, which is blast frozen in 55-gallon poly-drums and only available in full container loads. The shelf life of frozen organic Hibiscus liquid extract is 1 year.
