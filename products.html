Our Products

Hibiscus Extract Powder

We specialize in supplying Certified Organic Hibiscus extract powder to the food and beverage industry. Our latest version is 100% pure concentrated Hibiscus and is instantly soluble in ice cold water. The resulting crystal-clear beverage possesses the robust flavor and dark burgundy-red color of real Hibiscus and will closely match brewed Hibiscus tea made from boiled dried flowers. Organic Hibiscus Extract Powder has a 1-year shelf life and is packaged in 25 kg poly-lined small fiber drums for easy storage.

Hibiscus Concentrated Liquid Extract

Our 40 Brix concentrated liquid Organic Hibiscus extract is blast frozen in 55-gallon poly-drums and only available in full container loads. The shelf life of frozen Organic Hibiscus liquid extract is 1 year.
